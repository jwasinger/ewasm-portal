EVM384 - Update 3

 Posted by Ewasm Team on October 16, 2020
  • Releases
This is the third post in our series on EVM384, a proposal to add opcodes for 384bit modular arithmetic to the EVM. In this post, we present updates on the EVM384 implementation of BLS12831 pairing and present benchmarks for a new variant of the EVM384 interface which modifies the endianness of EVM384 arguments to match the EVM.
